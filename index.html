Badminton Club

Who are we?

UW Badminton Club provides organized badminton play, camaraderie, coaching, and tournaments for UW-Madison affiliates.

How can I get involved?

This year, our club meets on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Sundays from 10-12pm @ the Nick on the 3rd floor. To get involved, show up and ask for a club executive. Alternatively, contact the club's instagram account here and ask about joining.
